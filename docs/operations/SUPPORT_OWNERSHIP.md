# Support Ownership

Last updated: 2026-06-15

## Status

Support ownership is evidenced complete for private beta.

Evidence:
`docs/operations/evidence/support-ownership/2026-06-04-support-ownership.md`

## Owner

James Walsh

## Primary Support Channel

`support@complyeur.com`

## Secondary Support Channel

In-app beta feedback flow, reviewed through the internal `/admin/feedback`
triage surface.

## Coverage

### Beta Period

Founder-monitored support.

James Walsh owns beta support coverage and monitors the primary support mailbox
directly during the beta period. The inbox is reviewed at least twice per
business day, with increased launch-day checks during the first 12 hours after
tester invitations.

## Response Targets

### Critical Issues

Within 4 hours.

### High Priority

Within 1 business day.

### Normal Support

Within 2 business days.

## Escalation Process

### Critical incidents

Immediate investigation by James Walsh. Critical incidents include login
outages, production availability issues, and failures that block beta users from
using core workflows.

### Data loss or tenant isolation concerns

Immediate investigation by James Walsh. These issues are treated as security and
data-protection incidents until proven otherwise.

### Billing failures

Same-day review by James Walsh. Payment failures, subscription errors, and
Stripe webhook concerns are reviewed against billing logs and Stripe dashboard
state.

## Communication

Users receive acknowledgement within the applicable response target.

Critical and high-priority issues receive direct follow-up until resolution or
until a mitigation, workaround, or next update time has been communicated.

## Notes

- During beta, support ownership is assigned to James Walsh rather than a shared
  or unowned inbox.
- This assignment satisfies the private-beta support ownership blocker in
  `docs/operations/evidence/EVIDENCE_STATUS.md`.
- No support rota is required while founder-monitored support is in effect.
- If support volume increases or beta coverage expands beyond founder-monitored
  hours, this document must be updated with named backup coverage and handoff
  rules before the expanded beta starts.
